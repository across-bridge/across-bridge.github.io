What is Across Bridge?

Across is an intent-based cross-chain protocol for moving assets between supported networks through its transfer app and developer APIs. Its developer documentation explains integration paths for swaps, bridge actions and builders.

How Across Bridge works

  1. Open the appUse the primary action to visit the official Across interface and connect a wallet you control.
  2. Choose a routeSelect the token, origin network, destination network and amount supported by the app.
  3. Review the quoteCheck the destination amount, route details, fees and estimated timing before signing.
  4. Confirm and trackApprove token spending if prompted, sign the transaction and monitor transfer status in the app.

Across Bridge FAQ

Is Across Bridge the official Across app?

No. This page is a routing page; use app.across.to for the official transfer interface and verify the domain before connecting.

What can I do with Across Bridge?

You can route to Across to move supported tokens between supported chains when the official app provides a quote for your route.

Do I need a wallet for Across Bridge?

Yes. Cross-chain transfers require a compatible self-custody wallet, token balance on the origin chain and gas for the transaction.

Does Across Bridge set fees?

No. This landing page does not quote or set fees; review all amounts, route details and network costs inside the official app before signing.

Is Across Bridge risk-free?

No. Bridges involve smart contract, network and user-error risk; review the ethereum.org bridge overview before transferring significant value.
