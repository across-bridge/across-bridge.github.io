How it works

Use Across Bridge in three steps

The flow starts and ends in your wallet: choose the route, review the live quote, then approve the transaction you want to send.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect a Web3 wallet. Across Bridge does not ask you to create an exchange account.

2

Pick route and asset

Select the source chain, destination chain and token. Common routes include Ethereum to Arbitrum when liquidity is available.

3

Review and confirm

Check the live route, expected destination asset, timing and costs in the app, then sign the transaction from your wallet.

FAQ

Across Bridge questions

01What is Across Bridge?

Across Bridge is a non-custodial DeFi app for bridging and swapping assets across supported chains. The official crosschain app provides the live route and wallet transaction details before you sign.

02Which assets and chains can I use?

Available assets and chains depend on current liquidity and route support in the app. You can review the local overview on the supported chains page before opening the dashboard.

03How long does Across Bridge take?

Transfer time depends on the selected chains, token, wallet confirmation and route conditions. The app shows the timing estimate before you confirm.

04What does it cost?

The cost depends on network fees, route liquidity and the transaction you choose. Across Bridge shows the live quote and destination result in the app.

05Is Across Bridge safe to use?

No bridge is free from smart-contract, liquidity or wallet risk. Review the route, confirm the URL, check every wallet prompt and use the safety guidance in the app before sending funds.
